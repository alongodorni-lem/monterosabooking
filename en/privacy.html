Privacy and cookies

Summary notice for the Macugnaga Booking portal – Experiences at the foot of Monte Rosa.

Controller

For requests about the booking portal: macugnagabooking@gmail.com.

Technical cookies

The site uses technical cookies required for operation (for example saving the cookie-banner preference in localStorage).

Third-party services

  • Online booking system — search, experiences list, map and payment. During booking, cookies and data processing needed to manage the reservation may be used. The map may load third-party map content.
  • Availability in coming days — the site may query the booking-system API (via a proxy on the portal domain) to show bookable activities in the immediately following days. The response may be stored temporarily in the browser’s localStorage to limit requests.
  • Google Fonts — loading of Open Sans and Fraunces fonts.

We do not use our own advertising profiling tools. Any third-party cookies depend on the services listed above.

Booking data

Data entered in the booking form are processed to manage the reservation, payments and communication with guides/operators, according to the purposes of the online booking service.

Responsibility for experiences

Information, prices and availability on the booking portal are provided by the experience operators. After booking online you will receive the organisers’ direct contacts for any further information. Lem s.r.l. is in no way responsible for the management of the activities.

Your rights

You can request information about processing of your data by writing to macugnagabooking@gmail.com.

This page is for information. For details on data processing during booking, also see the notice of the online booking system used.
