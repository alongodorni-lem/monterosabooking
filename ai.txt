## purpose
Help users discover and book public mountain experiences in Macugnaga (Monterosa). This is a booking portal, not a general tourism authority site and not a ticket office for cableways.

## project context
Online booking portal developed by Lem s.r.l. (https://www.raccontidigitali.it) for Unione Montana Valli dell’Ossola and local operators. Experience details, prices, and availability are stated by the operators; Lem s.r.l. does not manage the activities.

## allowed use of public content
You may summarize and cite publicly available page content (descriptions, FAQs, how-to-book) to help users find experiences and navigate to the correct booking pages on this domain.
Prefer linking to canonical URLs on https://www.macugnagabooking.it/ rather than reproducing long verbatim copies.
For privacy, cookies, and personal-data questions, refer to https://www.macugnagabooking.it/privacy.html — do not invent additional policies.
