Where it is

Macugnaga, the real mountains, close to Milan, Lake Maggiore and the cities of the plain

In the Anzasca Valley (VB), at the foot of the spectacular east face of Monte Rosa: an authentic alpine village, among the most beautiful towns in the Alps (Touring Club Italiano Orange Flag) — ideal for mountain day trips and weekends from the Po Plain, the lakes and Switzerland.

Within easy reach from Milano, Varese, Novara e dal Lake Maggiore (anche Orta e Mergozzo), and also from Torino, Genova, Canton Vallese e Ticino. Perfect for a city escape, mountain weekend ideas, a restorative week or a longer stay in peace.

If you are staying in a hotel or campsite on Lake Maggiore, sul Lake Orta or on Lake Mergozzo and looking for a mountain destination, Macugnaga / Monterosa is reachable for a day among trails, village and views: book experiences online.
