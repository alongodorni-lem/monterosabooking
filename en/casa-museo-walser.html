Why visit

Walser history, a step from Monte Rosa

The Ancient Walser House Museum of Borca (Alts Walserhüüs Van Zer Burfuggu) collects, catalogues and preserves everyday objects of Macugnaga’s Walser people.

It is housed in the 17th-century parish house in Borca: one of the finest traditional dwellings in the area, almost untouched over the centuries. Most artefacts were donated by Macugnaga locals themselves.

An authentic experience for families, schools, couples and anyone who wants to understand the village beyond the scenery — also on a Macugnaga weekend or a day trip from the plain: book now and secure your place.

Who were the Walser

The Walser in Macugnaga

From the Middle Ages, small groups of Alemannic settlers left the Upper Valais and, through alpine passes — including the Monte Moro Pass — founded Macugnaga.

In the House Museum you find the concrete memory of that community: crafts, household objects, bread, the mine. An essential piece to understand the village you are visiting.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about the Walser House Museum

How do I book the visit online?

Click Book now: the online booking form for House Museum entry opens. Complete payment and receive confirmation by email.

Where is it?

In the Borca hamlet of Macugnaga (VB), in the Anzasca Valley at the foot of Monte Rosa, in the 17th-century parish house.

How long does it last and what do you see?

About one hour: basement with prints and exhibitions; ground floor with kitchen, entrance and stube; upper floor with crafts, bread and mining documents.

Is it suitable for families and children?

Yes: concrete rooms and stories of life in the past. Ideal also for schools and groups.

Can I visit outside opening hours?

Yes, by appointment. Booking online locks date and entry; for special needs contact the museum on 347 9842329.

Why is it worth it on a Macugnaga weekend?

It is the perfect cultural complement to walks, the mine and lifts: in one hour you meet the village’s Walser soul. Ideal also on a day trip from Milan, Varese or Novara. Plan the weekend.
