## What this site is
Public booking portal for mountain experiences in Macugnaga (Monterosa, Ossola Valley, Piedmont, Italy). Visitors can browse and book guided activities, cultural visits, and themed itineraries online (payment via the booking system). Content and availability are provided by local operators.

The online booking system is developed by Lem s.r.l. (https://www.raccontidigitali.it) for Unione Montana Valli dell’Ossola and local operators. Lem s.r.l. is not responsible for the management of the activities themselves.

Translated pages (EN/FR/DE) are automated translations from the Italian original; Italian remains the source of truth.
