FAQ

Frequently asked questions about the weekend

How to organise a weekend in Macugnaga Monte Rosa?

Accommodation + local cuisine + one or two experiences booked online (nature, Walser House, mine). Ideal as a mountain weekend idea from Milan, Lake Maggiore, Varese or Novara.

How long from Milan or Varese?

About 1.5–2 hours from Varese/Novara and about 2 hours from Milan; also reachable from hotels and campsites on Lake Maggiore, Orta and Mergozzo. Details on City escape.

What not to miss?

Sunset on the Rosa, a nature experience, Walser House and/or gold mine, and the lifts if open.
